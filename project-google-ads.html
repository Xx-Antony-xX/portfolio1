PPC Campaign Management

Google Ads ROI Optimization

Optimized ad spend for a local business, achieving a 4x Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) within just 3 months.

Project Overview

The Challenge

A local service-based business was spending a significant budget on Google Ads but seeing very little return. Their Cost Per Click (CPC) was high, and the leads generated were often unqualified. They needed a complete overhaul of their PPC strategy to maximize their budget and drive quality leads.

The Solution

I conducted a deep-dive audit of their existing account and restructured their campaigns for maximum efficiency.

  • Keyword Research: Identified high-intent, long-tail keywords with lower competition to reduce CPC.
  • Negative Keywords: Implemented a robust negative keyword list to filter out irrelevant traffic.
  • Landing Page Optimization: Redesigned landing pages to improve Quality Score and conversion rates.
  • A/B Testing: Continuously tested ad copy and bidding strategies to optimize performance.
